Tennant Creek Transport Inc.

About

Primary transportation facility of the Tennant Creek region established in 2012 that aims to provide quality service to local people. The purpose of the association therefore is to provide a public benefit by providing assistance for those people who are in need of relief from sickness, suffering, disability and helplessness within the Barkly region by providing trasnportation services.
